Who We Are

The Duneburg Association is a humanitarian organization dedicated to giving displaced people the tools they need to reclaim their lives with dignity. Our mission begins with real, tangible support: helping refugees find safety, work, and a sense of belonging through practical solutions, not promises.

The Bloom Project

The Bloom Project is our first step — a hands-on program to relocate refugees into €1 homes across Italy. We assist participants with everything from work visa applications and job placement to housing registration and basic digital training. Through ongoing support and language education, the Bloom Project helps them integrate, earn, and thrive — turning survival into stability, and exile into opportunity.
